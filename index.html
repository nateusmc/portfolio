About Me:

I am a full stack web developer from the Dallas, Texas area. I am most fluent in the MERN stack (MongoDB, Express, React, Node.js), however I love to learn and am always trying new things and searching for the next challenge so I am definitely not limited to just this stack. My ideal work environment would be somewhere where I can contribute to a meaninful project that impacts people's lives in a great way. I love working in teams and have leadership abilites as well from my experience in the United States Marine Corps.


In my free time I like to learn new development languages, libraries and frameworks, as well as play some strategy video games in my down time such as HearthStone. I like that these games keep my mind moving and make me think as well as giving me some down time to relax so I don't get burnt out trying to learn so many new things and master old skills. I also love spending time in the mountains and off-road looking at stars, wildlife, and enjoying nature in general.


The thing I love most about development is that I never get bored and am constantly facing new challenges and bettering myself. There is always something out there to learn in this industry and I love the challenge of learning as much of it as I can and mastering the technologies I enjoy most. I also have a backround in SEO, online marketing, ad campaign management such as facebook ads, etc which directly correlates into my web development experience as the 2 skillsets mesh together quite nicely.
